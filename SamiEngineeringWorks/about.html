About Us

Engineering Solutions, Project Execution, Clean Energy, Sustainability & Consultancy.


Your Partner for Complete Solutions and Comprehensive Support.


For more than 30 years we at SEW have been providing innovative and reliable solutions for standards - based protocols. Be it High Voltage & Low Voltage Electrical Projects for Indian Railways, Industrial Units and Solar unit installations, we have delivered projects with full customer satisfaction.

The scope of work ranges from consulting and specialized solutions for Power System Projects, Power System Protection, Automation, Communications, Controls, to retrofits and upgrades of microgrids and region-wide power systems. Every solution is custom engineered and backed by our 24X7 technical support team. Partner with our engineers for projects to specify, design, implement, test, and commission power systems, using latest technology to ensure reliability and efficiency.
